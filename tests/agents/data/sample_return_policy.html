Return & Exchange Policy

Returns

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • All tags must be attached
  • Original packaging required
  • Receipt or proof of purchase required

Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive your return.

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

For defective or damaged items, we will provide a prepaid return label.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Personalized or custom items
  • Gift cards
  • Intimate apparel
  • Final sale items

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for size and color only within 30 days of delivery.

Free exchange shipping labels are provided for all exchanges.
